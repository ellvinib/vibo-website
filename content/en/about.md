## Vincent's Story

### Headline
**Founded by Vincent Bouillart**

### Body
VIBO IT Consulting was founded by Vincent Bouillart, an experienced IT professional with a deep-rooted passion for technology and innovation. After years of experience in the IT sector, Vincent identified a clear need in the market: businesses want to leverage the latest technologies like AI, but often lack the expertise or capacity to make it happen on their own.

With VIBO IT Consulting, Vincent brings that expertise to businesses in Belgium and the Netherlands. His approach is hands-on and personal — not a big consultancy machine, but an engaged partner who thinks along, builds along, and grows along with your organization.

> "I believe technology is only truly valuable when it solves real problems. My mission is to make AI and IT accessible to every business, regardless of size."
> — **Vincent Bouillart**, Founder of VIBO IT Consulting

## Our Vision on AI

### Headline
**AI as an Accelerator, Not a Replacement**

### Body
At VIBO, we see artificial intelligence not as a magic solution, but as a powerful accelerator for existing business processes. Our AI vision rests on three pillars:

#### 1. Pragmatic, Not Hype-Driven
We implement AI where it genuinely adds value. Not every challenge requires AI — and that's perfectly fine. We provide honest advice about what works and what doesn't.

#### 2. Human-Centered
AI should support people, not replace them. Our solutions are designed to make teams more productive and free them from repetitive work, so they can focus on what truly matters.

#### 3. Ethical & Transparent
We build AI solutions that are explainable, fair, and compliant with privacy legislation. Trust is the foundation of every successful AI implementation.
